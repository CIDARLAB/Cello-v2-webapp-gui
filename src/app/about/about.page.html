This is Cello v2, the continuation of the Cello genetic circuit design automation software. The original version of Cello was published in Science in 2016:

Nielsen, A. A. K.; Der, B. S.; Shin, J.; Vaidyanathan, P.; Paralanov, V.; Strychalski, E. A.; Ross, D.; Densmore, D.; Voigt, C. A. Genetic Circuit Design Automation. Science 2016, 352 (6281), aac7341.
